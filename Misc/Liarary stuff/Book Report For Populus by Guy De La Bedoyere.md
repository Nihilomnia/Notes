
# General Premise
Populus is an informational/ non-fiction book that focuses on the life of the Romans, providing insight on what Ancient Rome truly was like. Through the user of the words of Tacitus, Seneca, Martial and a substantial amount of other parties including the everyday Roman.


# My thoughts
Overall i thought this was a very interesting book that captivated how dark life as a roman during those times were, from  the violent politics, the violence, and the staggering inequality, participated by the hyper-rich elite to the lowest slaves.

To those who are interested with history i would definitely recommend you read this.
